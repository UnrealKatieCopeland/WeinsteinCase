Weinstein v. Copeland – Judicial Overview

This portal contains the key documents and statutory frameworks necessary for the Court to perform its ministerial duties under the Fair Housing Act (FHA), Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA), Texas Fair Housing Act (TFHA), and Texas Property Code.

The Court’s Three Ministerial Questions

  1. Is Defendant a person with disabilities under FHA/ADA? → Yes. Medical documentation from UT Southwestern physicians establishes multiple chronic, life-limiting conditions.
  2. Were accommodation requests made? → Yes. Written requests were submitted on March 5, June 27, June 30, August 14, and September 11, 2025.
  3. Was the nonrenewal issued in retaliation? → Yes. Nonrenewal issued August 26, 2025 – only 12 days after the August 14 FHA accommodation notice.
Each answer is supported by documentary evidence already filed. No credibility determinations or evidentiary hearing are required.
