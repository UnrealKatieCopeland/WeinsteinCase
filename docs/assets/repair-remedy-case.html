1. Repair & Remedy Elements

Elements the Court Must Decide

  • Serious condition: Is there a condition in Unit 2145 that materially affects the physical health or safety of an ordinary tenant?
  • Notice: Did the tenant give proper written notice and a reasonable opportunity to repair?
  • Failure to repair: Did the landlord fail to make a diligent effort to repair or complete licensed remediation within a reasonable time?
  • Damages / consequences: Did the tenant incur costs or losses because the unit was uninhabitable?
  • Relief: What repairs, rent-related relief, and costs should the Court order under the Property Code?
